The Foundation

Manifestation begins with
balance across 6 areas of life

Manifestation is not about attracting quick fixes. The deepest level of manifestation is grounding yourself across six pillars — because a life in balance naturally attracts what it needs.

💚

Health

Vitality is your natural state. We don't focus on illness — we attract radiant physical and mental wellbeing by focusing entirely on how we want to feel. Your body responds to what your mind consistently holds.

🌟

Self

Everything begins here. Your beliefs, your identity, your self-worth — they silently shape every relationship, financial choice, and life decision. This is the most foundational pillar of all. Deep work here corrects everything else.

💫

Money

Money is energy in motion — not a goal to chase, but a flow to invite. We teach you to address your deepest desires directly. The truth is, money is one of the easiest things to manifest once you shift your inner story about it.

🤝

Relationship

Every connection is a mirror. We focus on intentional connections and perception correction — shifting how you see yourself and others so that every relationship, personal or professional, begins to transform from within.

🚀

Career

Stop optimising for short-term wins. Long-term growth that connects to your life's purpose — not just a salary or title. When your work aligns with your calling, everything you create carries a different kind of energy.

✨

Wealth

Clarity about what you truly want to build is not materialism — it's honesty. Wealth mindset addresses the material clarity that drives human ambition. Know what you want. Build toward it intentionally.

Questions

Things people often wonder
before stepping in

What exactly is Cosmos and who is it for?
+
Cosmos is a daily manifestation portal — a community, a practice system, a course library, and a space to grow. It is open to anyone above 21 who is ready to take their inner work seriously. Whether you are just beginning your manifestation journey or have been on this path for years, Cosmos gives you the structure, the community, and the daily rituals to make it real and lasting.
What are the daily practices and how do I choose?
+
Cosmos has a curated library of 50+ manifestation practices — from Gratitude Writing and Water Manifestation to 777 Scripting, 369 Method, Visualisation, and more. Each practice is aligned to one of your 6 life pillars. You browse the library, choose what resonates with where you are, and add them to your daily portal. Start with 2 or 3 — build naturally from there.
What are the monthly live group sessions?
+
Every month, Stardust members gather live on Zoom — not as a class, but as a collective. It is a space to align, reflect, share, and deepen your practice alongside the entire tribe. These sessions are recorded and added to the Stardust Archives so you can revisit them anytime.
Is the 3-day pass really free? What happens after?
+
Yes — completely free, no payment required. When you sign up, you get 3 days to explore the portal at the free access level. After 3 days, your access remains but at a limited level. To continue your full practice, you choose Nebula or Stardust. All your progress and data stay with you regardless.
I have tried things before and always quit. How is this different?
+
That is exactly who Cosmos is built for. The reason most people quit is not lack of willpower — it is lack of structure and community. Cosmos gives you both. Daily streak tracking, a tribe of 2,900+ who show up alongside you, challenges that create group accountability, and a practice system that makes consistency feel natural rather than forced.
Can I access Cosmos on my phone?
+
Yes. Cosmos is built mobile-first. Open cosmos.epicrecap.com on your phone and add it to your home screen — it works like a native app on both iOS and Android, with no App Store download required. You can show up anywhere, anytime.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
+
Yes, always. Cancel from your profile settings at any time. Your access continues until the end of your billing period, then steps down to the free level. All your practice history, streaks, and progress stay with you. The portal is always here when you are ready to return.
